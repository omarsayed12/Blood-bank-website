Way To Prevent Diseases

Infectious diseases are diseases that can be transmitted from one person to another, or from various pollution sources to humans, and this source may be a person infected with the disease, or an infected animal, and infection can also occur as a result of eating contaminated food, or exposure to environmental factors that contain Viruses, bacteria, fungi, or parasites. Infectious diseases are accompanied by the emergence of many symptoms, including: high body temperature, loss of appetite and a feeling of general weakness in the body, as well as feeling pain in the muscles, inability to move well, and difficulty breathing ... 'Madam Net' informs you of methods of prevention from Infectious diseases, and methods of transmission of these diseases in the following, and in order to prevent infectious diseases, a number of instructions can be followed, namely: 1- Wash hands frequently, with soap and water for at least 20 seconds. 2- Not sharing personal belongings with others, such as toothbrushes, towels, razors, napkins and nail clippers; Because these tools can be one of the sources of infection with bacteria, viruses and fungi. 3- Covering the mouth when coughing or sneezing. 4- Do not touch the eyes, ear and nose with the hands; Because this causes the microbes to be easily transferred inside the body, and thus the infection spread. 5- Use safe cooking practices; Because microbes grow on all foodstuffs, food must be stored in the refrigerator two hours after preparation, as cooling helps stop the growth of most microbes. 6- The need to deal with caution with animals, as the infection that is transmitted from animals to humans is the most common, so it is necessary to conduct regular checks for pets that are raised at home, and to make sure that young children stay away from animal feces.
